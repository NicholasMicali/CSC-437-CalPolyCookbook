Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • Day old rice
    2 cups
  • 2 whole eggs
  • peas and carrots
    1 Cup
  • green onions
    1/2 Cups

Instructions:

  • Start by heating a large pan with olive oil on high
  • add rice and stir to brown the rice
  • add fish sause and spices of choice
  • add egg to rice and stir
  • add vegtables, turn the heat down and stir
  • garnish with green onions and serve
