Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Pasta sheets
    1 Box
  • Ricotta chesse
    5 Tablespoons
  • Tomato sauce
    1 Cup
  • Ground beef
    1 Cup
  • Parsley
    2 Cups

Instructions:

  • Start by Boiling water in a medium pot
  • cook beef in a large pan until golden brown
  • Add pasta sheets to boiling water and add salt to taste
  • cover pot and stir occasionlly for 15 minutes
  • Strain pasta and mix the sauces with the beef
  • layer sheets with cheese and meat sauce in between
  • Bkae on 350 for 20 minutes
  • top with cheese and serve
