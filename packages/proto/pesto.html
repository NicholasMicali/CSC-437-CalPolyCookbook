Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pene Pasta
    1 Box
  • Olive Oil
    5 Tablespoons
  • Pine Nuts
    1 Cup
  • Parsley
    2 Cups

Instructions:

  • Start by Boiling water in a medium pot
  • While waiting, mix pine nuts, olive oil, and parsley in a blender
  • Add pasta to boiling water and add salt to taste
  • cover pot and stir occasionlly for 15 minutes
  • Strain pasta and combine with pesto
  • let stand for 5 minutes and serve
