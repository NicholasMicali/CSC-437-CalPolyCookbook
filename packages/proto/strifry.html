Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • Cheicken Breast
    2 Boneless Breasts
  • Bell Peppers
    3 Medium sized
  • Brockoli
    1 Cup
  • Carrots
    1 Cups

Instructions:

  • Start by heating a large pan with olive oil
  • dice and season chicken breast with spices of choice
  • add chiken to pan, fliping when golden brown sear
  • add vegtables and turn the heat down
  • add sauce of choice and stir
  • let stand for 5 minutes and serve
